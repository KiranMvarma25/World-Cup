India vs England

India Knocked out the Defending Champions

On the bowling pitch, India has picked up the sixth victory in a row, as they defeated defending champions England by 100 runs. After setting a target of 230 runs, the bowling attack of Bumrah, Shami, Kuldeep and Jadeja succeeded in restricting the English side to 129.

Venue: Ekana Sports Stadium, Lucknow

  • England won the toss and opt to Bowl.
  • English bowlers had a great start, they got the early wickets of gill, kohli, iyer.
  • Once again Captain Rohit(87) guided the innings with Rahul.
  • After the dismissal of Rahul(39), Rohit build the innings with Surya(49).
  • At last India managed to score a fighting total in this bowling track.
  • Shami with 4wkts and Bumrah with 3wkts didn't even given chance to English batters.
  • They are getting the wickets continuosly for India.
  • Kuldeep bowled a magical delivery to dismiss ENG captain Buttler.
  • On the bowling track India finally defended the low total.

Scores:

  • IND - 229/9 (50 overs)
  • ENG - 129 (34.5 overs)

Player of the Match : Rohit Sharma
