India vs South Africa

Kohli ton, Jadeja five-fer flatten South Africa

King Virat Kohli on his birthday joined Legend Sachin Tendulkar at the top of the ODI centurions list as India posted 326 and then had another head from their five-fanged bowling attack bury South Africa under this mountain of runs. Ravindra Jadeja waved the spin flag with gusto, finishing with 5 for 33 in a dominating 243-run win for the hosts which assures them of a top of the league standings finish for a second straight World Cup.
