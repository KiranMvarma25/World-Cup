India vs Afghanistan

HITman show

Rohit slams record World Cup ton as India crush Afghanistan by 8 wickets. Rohit Sharma oozed class and elegance en route to a recording-breaking hundred, leading India to an emphatic eight-wicket win over Afghanistan in the ODI World Cup. Afghanistan came as the underdogs in the tournament but they managed to score a fighting total of 272 against the quality bowling line up. In the chase India had a good start.
